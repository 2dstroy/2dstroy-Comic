One Fish
Makes a Splash

One Fish was just trying to get to school on time. What followed was a wild dash through the ocean — a surprise encounter with a very grumpy crab, a mad swim through kelp forests, and an arrival that nobody expected. A story about showing up, even when the current is against you. Perfect for readers of all ages and anyone who's ever been five minutes late. Collect the issue as an NFT on the Ronin Network.

Meet 2dstroy

I'm 2dstroy, the artist behind RoninComics — an independent comic studio bringing hand-drawn stories on-chain as NFTs on the Ronin Network. Every comic starts with a simple idea and a blank page, and turns into something you can own.

I've been drawing since I could hold a pencil and telling stories since before that. My work is hand-crafted, panel by panel, with characters inspired by everyday moments and the wild imagination in between. Each issue is minted as an NFT on Ronin — collect the story, own a piece of it. Find me on social — I love hearing from readers and collectors.
